A block of mass 2 kg is pulled along a horizontal surface by a force of 10 N. If the coefficient of kinetic friction is 0.2, what is the work done by friction when the block moves 5 m?
  • A) -10 J
  • B) -20 J
  • C) -30 J
  • D) -40 J

Hint (Level 1):

What forces are doing work in this scenario?

Solution:

  1. Identify the forces acting on the block: F_friction = μ × N
  2. Calculate work done by friction: W = F × d × cos(θ)

Final Answer: Option B
