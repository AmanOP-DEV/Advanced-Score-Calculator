Your MVP is a web-based practice system for JEE preparation that converts your study material into a structured, digital question bank. You upload or paste content from DPPs or modules, and an AI API processes it into clean questions with options, answers, hints, and solutions. This removes the friction of using physical books or PDFs and gives you a consistent, usable format inside your app.

Once the questions are stored, the platform lets you practice them in a focused interface where you solve one question at a time. You can choose to answer, use hints, or view solutions without breaking your flow. The system automatically tracks key behaviors such as time taken, correctness, hint usage, and whether you relied on solutions, giving a more realistic and controlled practice experience than traditional methods.

The core value of the MVP is not just solving questions but understanding how you solve them. After each session, it provides simple insights like accuracy, speed, and weak areas, helping you identify patterns in your preparation. The AI API handles structuring the content, while your app ensures validation, storage, practice flow, and performance tracking, making the entire system reliable and actionable.
